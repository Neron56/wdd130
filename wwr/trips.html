Beginners Rapids

A perfect place to start. Only a 2 hour, 10 mile trip down one of the softer rapids here in Utah. Still plenty of excitement to be had here. Especially if you're bringing first timers or are one yourself.

Canyon Reserve Rapids

More of a medium pace, For those who want a bit more of a challenge compared to the Beginners Rapids. 4 hours and 13 miles. Prepare for beautiful scenic tour down what sights Utah has to offer.

White Water Canyon

Our longest and most thrilling tour of them all! come join us for the long haul of 10 hours, covering just over 18 miles of the best Utah has to offer, This is for the experienced who love to see and feel it all.
